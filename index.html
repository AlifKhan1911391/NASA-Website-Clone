NASA's SpaceX
Crew-10

NASA and SpaceX are targeting no earlier than 6:05 p.m. EDT, Friday, Aug. 8, for the unlocking of the agency's SpaceX Crew-10 mission from the International Space Station. Crew-10 will be the first mission to splash down off the California coast for the agency's Commercial Crew Program.
